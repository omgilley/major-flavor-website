Our Story

Woman-owned, community-fed, major flavor guaranteed

Born from the Kitchen, Built for the Community

Major Flavor isn't just a restaurant — it's a love language. Located in the heart of Moss Point, Mississippi, we've built a reputation on doing things right: fresh ingredients, real soul food recipes, and a vibe that makes you feel at home the second you walk in.

We start early — real early. Our kitchen fires up at 5am, because great food doesn't wait. Whether you're grabbing a Crack of Dawn breakfast before work or pulling up on a Friday night for a wing tray and hookah, we've got something for every version of your day.

We're woman-owned and proud. This spot was built on hustle, heart, and a whole lot of flavor. Every item on our menu is made with intention — from our homemade mac & cheese to our peach cobbler, nothing here is an afterthought.
